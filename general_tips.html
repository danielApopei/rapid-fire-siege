Rapid Fire Siege Maps Operators General Tips Movement & Aim Settings Tips

General Tips

Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dPg0MV79SAY

General

  • prefire more! (tap-fire common angles)
  • avoid crouch walking
  • learn how to quick peek
  • care less (play more smoothly, relaxed)
  • turn sens down

Attack

  • Capitao - smoke ceiling for one-way smoke
  • Azami - one-way angle
  • Ram - put drone right on Mira mirror
  • Azami - destroy floors, use vertical floorboards to attach Azami panel anywhere
  • Azami - pixel peek with V-shape (press yourself up against wall) and then another on top
  • Thatcher - throw 2 grenades (one after 0.5sec) to counter Kaid trick
  • Ash - ash the top-left or top-right corner of barricade to take no damage and enter quickly
  • Thermite - get around mute jammers by placing thermite on the top side of wall (rapel)
  • Twitch & Zero - infinite range!

Defense

  • shoot window glass (4 spots) to hear people rapelling
  • barricade head level - 2nd buckle (top one)
  • barricade peek - shoot wood side
